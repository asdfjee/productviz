How It Works

We've made getting your custom visualization as easy as possible.

Our Simple 3-Step Process

  1. Step 1: You Send Us Your Photos.
    You start by uploading a photo of your space. Then, you either upload a photo of the exact furniture you like or simply describe the item or style you're imagining.

  2. Step 2: Our Designers Get to Work.
    This is where the magic happens! A real person from our design team takes your request. Using our special software, we create a realistic 3D version of the furniture and place it into your photo. We carefully match the lighting, shadows, and scale to make it look like it's really there.

  3. Step 3: You Receive Your Custom Visualization.
    Within 2-3 business days, we send the final, high-quality image or video directly to your email or phone. You can then see exactly how the new furniture will look in your home!
