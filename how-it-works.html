How PRODUCT VIZ Works

Our Process

Our service combines your photos with cutting-edge AI technology and a touch of human artistry to create a compelling visualization. Here’s a breakdown of the steps:

  1. You Submit Your Order: You provide us with a clear photo of your space and a photo of the furniture or product you're interested in. The product photo can be from a retail website or one you've taken yourself.

  2. AI-Powered Analysis: Our system analyzes your room's photo to understand its dimensions, lighting, and perspective. It then analyzes the product photo to create a digital model of the item.

  3. Manual Review & Artistry: This is our secret sauce! A member of our design team reviews the AI-generated placement. They make crucial adjustments to lighting, shadows, and scale to ensure the final image looks as natural and believable as possible.

  4. Final Delivery: Once we're satisfied with the result, we render the final high-quality image or video. We then deliver it directly to you via the email or text number you provided. The entire process typically takes 2-3 business days.
